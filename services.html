Our Comprehensive Women's Health Services

At NurtureCare, we offer a full spectrum of obstetric and gynecological services designed to support you through every stage of womanhood. From preconception counseling to menopause management, our evidence-based approach combines medical excellence with compassionate care.

Prenatal & Pregnancy Care

Comprehensive Prenatal Care

  • Monthly checkups with fetal monitoring
  • Genetic screening and counseling
  • Gestational diabetes management
  • High-risk pregnancy supervision
  • Nutrition and lifestyle guidance
  • Birth plan development
  • 24/7 on-call obstetric support

Advanced Prenatal Diagnostics

  • First trimester screening (NT scan)
  • Anatomy ultrasound (20-week scan)
  • 3D/4D fetal imaging
  • Fetal echocardiography
  • Amniocentesis and CVS
  • Non-invasive prenatal testing (NIPT)
  • Growth scans and Doppler studies

Labor & Delivery Services

Natural Birth Support

  • Water birth options
  • Doula collaboration
  • Hypnobirthing techniques
  • Freedom of movement during labor
  • Minimal intervention approaches
  • Immediate skin-to-skin contact
  • Delayed cord clamping

Medical Birth Support

  • Epidural and pain management
  • Induction and augmentation
  • Cesarean section services
  • VBAC (Vaginal Birth After Cesarean)
  • Neonatal resuscitation
  • NICU coordination
  • Post-delivery care

Gynecological & Wellness Services

Routine Gynecological Care

  • Annual well-woman exams
  • Pap smears and HPV testing
  • STI screening and treatment
  • Contraceptive counseling
  • Menstrual disorder management
  • Menopause and perimenopause care
  • Bone density screening

Specialized Gynecological Services

  • Infertility evaluation and treatment
  • PCOS management
  • Endometriosis care
  • Uterine fibroid treatment
  • Pelvic floor therapy
  • Laparoscopic and hysteroscopic surgery
  • Colposcopy and LEEP procedures
